Mate

Description

Is a traditional South American caffeine-rich infused herbal drink.

Ingredients

  • Yerba mate
  • Hot water
  • "Bombilla" (metal straw)
  • Container called "mate"
  • Thermos

Steps

  1. Put yerba in the container
  2. Insert the bombilla
  3. Add hot water (not boiling)
  4. Share it whit yours friends!
Now live the experience of drinking mate!
