Argentine style barbecue

Description

Roast beef cooked on a grill, with sausages and roasted vegetables.

Ingredients

  • 1kg of Beef.
  • 2 pork sausages.
  • 2 blood sausages.
  • Potatoes, bell pepper and onion.
  • Grill salt.

Steps

  1. Salt the meat.
  2. Put the meat on the bone or fat side.
  3. When the juice starts to come out of the meat, turn it over.
  4. Once you turned it, place the sausages on the grill.
  5. Add the vegetables to the grill.
Now enjoy an delicious Argentinian barbecue!
